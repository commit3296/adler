# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal
appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment of any kind, public or private.
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Using this project's tooling to stalk, harass, or surveil people without
  authorization (see the README's "Ethics & responsible use").

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to
the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated
and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to
the circumstances.
